Our mission
Blessed is he who considers the poor; the Lord will deliver him in time of trouble. Psalm 41:1
Our mission

To serve every person with dignity and mercy.

We demonstrate Christ’s love through practical action and compassionate outreach, offering hope, support and encouragement to people experiencing homelessness.

Our service meets immediate needs with genuine care, creating a welcoming community where every person is treated with respect.

“The giver of every good and perfect gift has called upon us to mimic God’s giving, by grace, through faith.”
St Nicholas, Bishop of Myra

What we do

Practical care, offered with love.

From hot drinks to food parcels, every act of service is an expression of Christian compassion.

Our mission

To serve every person with dignity and mercy.

We are committed to demonstrating Christ’s love through practical action and compassionate outreach. We recognise the challenges faced by people experiencing homelessness and aim to be a source of hope, support and encouragement.

Our service meets immediate needs with genuine care, while creating a welcoming community where every person is treated with respect.

“The giver of every good and perfect gift has called upon us to mimic God’s giving, by grace, through faith.”
St Nicholas, Bishop of Myra

What we do

Practical care, offered with love.

From hot drinks to food parcels, every act of service is an expression of Christian compassion for our East London community.

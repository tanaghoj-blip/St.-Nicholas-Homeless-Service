Homeless Service

Give Today Our Mission

"We believe in treating every person with dignity, respect, and compassion, recognising the inherent worth and value of each individual."

Our mission is to serve our community with genuine care, addressing the immediate needs of individuals experiencing homelessness while offering pathways to long-term solutions. We recognise the challenges faced by those without stable housing and aim to be a source of hope, support, and empowerment throughout East London.

What We Do

Practical Support, Rooted in Compassion

Every initiative is a direct response to a need we've seen and heard from our neighbours in East London.

Food Drives & Collections

Regular collection campaigns gathering non-perishable food, hygiene products, clothing, and blankets for those who need them most.

Weekly Soup Kitchen

A warm meal and a welcoming table, served weekly in East London — because everyone deserves to eat with dignity.

Direct Distribution

Donations reach individuals directly and through our outreach table, meeting practical needs as they arise.

Our Story

About St Nicolas Homeless Service

Committed to demonstrating Christ's love through practical action and compassionate outreach in East London.

Why We Serve

Every Person Deserves a Place to Call Home

We are driven by the conviction that every individual deserves a place to call home and the opportunity to thrive. Through our Church Homeless Service, we strive to embody Christ's love by offering practical support, compassionate care, and the hope of a better future.

We recognise the challenges faced by individuals experiencing homelessness and aim to be a source of hope, support, and empowerment. Through our various initiatives, we strive to make a positive impact in the lives of those who find themselves without a stable place to call home in East London.

What We Hold To

Our Values

  • Dignity

    We treat every person with dignity, respect, and compassion, recognising the inherent worth and value of each individual we meet.

  • Genuine Care

    Our mission is to serve our community with genuine care, addressing immediate needs while walking alongside people toward long-term solutions.

  • Community

    From food drives to our weekly soup kitchen, we bring East London together to meet practical needs, side by side.

  • Hope

    We work toward a more inclusive and compassionate community, offering the hope of a better future to everyone we serve.

Our Parish

A Ministry of St Mary & St George Coptic Orthodox Church

St Nicolas Homeless Service is the outreach arm of St Mary & St George Coptic Orthodox Church, East London, serving under the pastoral guidance of Fr. Michael. Rooted in the Church's long tradition of charitable service, our volunteers give their time each week so that no one in our community faces hardship alone.

Join us in making a meaningful difference in the lives of those experiencing homelessness — together, let's work towards a more inclusive and compassionate community.
